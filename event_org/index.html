Baby Shower Decoration: Our baby shower decorations are simple, charming, and affordable rates. Whether you prefer traditional baby shower themes or a customized setup, we'll design it just the way you like it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Evenizers?

Evenizers is your one-stop solution for professional event decoration services, specializing in creating memorable experiences for all occasions, from birthdays and anniversaries to corporate events and baby showers. We handle everything from conceptualization to execution, ensuring your event space looks spectacular.

How can I book a decoration service?

Booking with Evenizers is simple! You can visit our website, browse our decoration packages, and fill out the inquiry form. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via phone or email to discuss your requirements. Our team will then get in touch to finalize the details and provide a quote.

Do you offer decoration services at home?

Yes, absolutely! Evenizers provides professional decoration services for a wide range of venues, including your home. Whether it's a small family gathering or a larger home party, our team can transform your space to fit your event's theme and make it truly special.

Which cities do you provide services in?

Evenizers proudly offers its services in major cities across India, including Pune, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, and Ahmedabad. Please check our website or contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Do I need to provide any materials for the decoration?

Generally, no. Evenizers provides all the necessary materials and equipment required for your decoration. Our packages are designed to be all-inclusive, ensuring a hassle-free experience for you. If there are any specific items you wish to incorporate, please discuss them with our team during the consultation.

How do I contact Evenizers for support?

For any support or inquiries, you can reach Evenizers through multiple channels. Visit our 'Contact Us' page on the website for phone numbers and email addresses. You can also connect with us via our social media platforms or use the live chat option available on our site during business hours.
